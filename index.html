by Leo De Firenze

DeFrost is a documentary about growing up in a little red house in a cold, harsh valley. This is a story about my mother's childhood. This film attempts to capture vignettes of her upbringing all family, friends, pets, and eventual depature from a freezing valley in Northern Italy all through beautiful cinematography and narration.
